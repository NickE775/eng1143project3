Introduction: The Travel Guide

This portfolio represents my journey through the ENGL 1143 semester. Think of this course as a trip, with each assignment serving as a stop along the way. As your guide, I will explain where I started, the challenges I faced, and how each project helped me grow as a writer and thinker.

The Semester Journey

At the beginning of the semester, my understanding of writing was mostly focused on structure and correctness. As the course progressed, I learned to think more deeply about audience, purpose, revision, and reflection. Each project pushed me to refine my ideas and connect my work to the Student Learning Outcomes (SLOs) outlined in the ENGL 1143 course description.

By the end of the semester, I have gained a stronger understanding of writing as a process rather than a single product. This portfolio demonstrates both my struggles and successes, showing how each step of the journey contributed to my overall growth.
